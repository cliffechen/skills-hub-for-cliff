# Prompt Library

Use one of these starter prompts and adapt it to the actual case.

## Standard Formula Reconstruction

- `Use formula-reconstruction to optimize the formula for {ASIN} based on upstream reports, keyword competition, market demand, and scientific evidence.`
- `Based on the upstream risk report and safe rebuild brief for {product}, find the best keyword anchor and build a competitively stronger formula.`
- `Read the Skill 1 and Skill 2 outputs for {ingredient}, then triangulate keyword data, consumer pain points, and scientific literature to produce 2-3 formula upgrade directions.`

## Keyword-Led

- `The anchor keyword is "{keyword}" with low SPR and PPC. Design a formula that wins on this keyword while staying within the safe ingredient pool.`
- `Compare keyword competition for the top 5 traffic terms of {ASIN} and recommend which keyword the formula should optimize around.`

## Demand-Led

- `Pull Amazon reviews, Reddit discussions, and web research for {product category}. What are the top 3 unmet consumer needs that a new formula could address?`
- `Users complain about pill size and slow onset. How should the formula and form factor change to address these pain points?`

## Science-Led

- `What does the literature say about combining Glucosamine HCl with Boswellia serrata vs Turmeric for joint support? Which combination has stronger evidence?`
- `Find the effective dose range for MSM in joint health studies and compare it to what top Amazon products are using.`

## Chinese Starters

- `请基于上游报告，从关键词竞争、市场需求和科学证据三个维度，输出配方优化方案。`
- `请分析 {ASIN} 的流量词竞争态势，找到蓝海锚定词，然后围绕这个词设计更强的成分组合。`
- `请从 Amazon 评论、Reddit 讨论和网络研究中提取用户痛点，然后结合科学文献给出配方升级建议。`
- `请对比 "glucosamine chondroitin" 和 "osteo biflex triple strength" 两个词的竞争数据，告诉我配方应该围绕哪个词做文章。`
