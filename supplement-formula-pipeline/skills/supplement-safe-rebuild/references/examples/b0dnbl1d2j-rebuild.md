# Example: B0DNBL1D2J Fatty15 Rebuild Report

Reuse this as a worked Chinese example. Replace product-specific facts when rebuilding another target.

## 1. Executive Summary

重建目标：尽量保留 `C15:0 / pentadecanoic acid` 所代表的 broad cellular wellness 概念，但避开 `FA15(TM)`、`fatty15`、`essential fatty acid` 确定性表达、`longevity` 和 `3X vs fish oil` 这类高风险叙事。  
最大可保留功能：围绕细胞稳态、日常代谢支持或更保守的全身健康方向。  
最大应避开风险：品牌原料、授权/专利故事、essentiality 绝对表述、优效比较。  
最大剩余不确定性：generic `C15:0` 的目标商业化路径是否会与现有专利/use claims 发生实质重叠，仍需法律复核。

## 2. Rebuild Brief Snapshot

- target market：Amazon US
- dosage form：capsule
- must-keep cues：single-ingredient simplicity、cellular wellness direction、clean positioning
- must-avoid elements：`FA15(TM)`、`fatty15`、`The Longevity Nutrient`、`essential fatty acid` certainty、`3X vs fish oil`
- desired benefit directions：broad cellular wellness、metabolic support、more conservative daily health support

## 5. Candidate Rebuild Directions

### Direction 1: Conservative Generic C15:0 Concept

- 核心功能：保留 `C15:0` 作为 generic chemistry concept 的极简方向
- 类别特征：single-ingredient simplicity
- 主动避开：`FA15(TM)`、`fatty15`、essentiality certainty、comparative claims
- claim posture：仅保留 broad wellness / daily support 级别
- commercial note：若采用此路，必须先做 supplier rights + patent counsel review

### Direction 2: Cellular Wellness Blend Concept

- 核心功能：把 broad cellular wellness 做成“多因素协同”而不是把全部故事压在 `C15:0` 上
- 类别特征：从单一 hero ingredient 转成 support system
- 主动避开：proprietary ingredient equivalence、longevity superiority story
- claim posture：偏向日常健康与细胞支持的保守结构/功能表述
- commercial note：更容易拉开与目标品牌的商业化距离

### Direction 3: Repositioned Daily Metabolic Support Concept

- 核心功能：保留更宽的日常代谢和生活方式支持方向
- 类别特征：clean-label, simple daily support
- 主动避开：anti-aging、repair、fish-oil superiority、fatty-liver-adjacent表达
- claim posture：更保守，围绕 general wellness 而非 longevity
- commercial note：这条路最不像竞品复刻，更适合合规优先 launch

## 6. Claim Guardrails

- 可以用：更保守的 `supports daily wellness`、`supports cellular health` 方向，前提是有支持性依据
- 尽量不用：`essential fatty acid` 的绝对表述、`longevity nutrient`、`repairs cells`、`slow aging`
- 不要碰：`FA15(TM)`、`fatty15`、`same as fatty15`、`3X vs fish oil`
- 如果涉及 liver 或 mitochondria 方向，必须预留更严格的合规和证据复核

## 7. Residual Risks & Required Review

- supplier identity, purity, and chain-of-title review for any `C15:0` source
- patent counsel review of DiscoverC15 / Seraphina portfolio before commercialization
- trademark clearance for product and ingredient callouts
- final Amazon listing and label compliance review
