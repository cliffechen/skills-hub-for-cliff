# Example: B09ZQ74495 Rebuild Report

Reuse this as a worked Chinese example. Replace product-specific facts when rebuilding another target.

## 1. Executive Summary

重建目标：尽量保留 `植物/海藻来源钙 + 750 mg 左右钙 + D3 + 多矿物骨支持` 卖点，但避开 `AlgaeCal®`、exact exclusive species/source story、以及骨密度增长类强 claim。  
最大可保留功能：日常补钙与骨骼营养支持。  
最大应避开风险：品牌源料、独家红藻故事、临床优效话术。  
最大剩余不确定性：若要继续讲“13 minerals from algae”，必须有新供应链文件支持。

## 2. Rebuild Brief Snapshot

- target market：Amazon US
- dosage form：veggie capsule
- must-keep cues：plant/algae calcium、bone support、multi-mineral feel
- must-avoid elements：`AlgaeCal®`、exact species story、bone density growth claims
- desired benefit directions：daily bone support、mineral support、bone matrix support

## 5. Candidate Rebuild Directions

### Direction 1: K-Free Plant Calcium Core

- 核心功能：基础补钙和骨骼营养支持
- 类别特征：植物/海藻来源钙
- 主动避开：`AlgaeCal®`、exact species 名、bone density claims
- claim posture：保守的 structure/function 语言
- commercial note：最接近基础版定位

### Direction 2: Bone Matrix Cofactor Version

- 核心功能：补钙 + 骨基质协同
- 类别特征：多矿物骨支持
- 主动避开：exclusive source story、clinical superiority story
- claim posture：bone matrix / connective support
- commercial note：更利于做自有差异化

### Direction 3: Utilization Upgrade Version

- 核心功能：矿物利用和骨支持协同
- 类别特征：多营养骨支持
- 主动避开：`K2 Vital®`、`Strontium Boost`、保证式临床表述
- claim posture：bone + mineral utilization support
- commercial note：若加入 `K2`，不能再保留基础版 `K-free` 逻辑

## 6. Claim Guardrails

- 可以用：`supports healthy bones`、`supports daily calcium intake`
- 尽量不用：`increase bone density`、`reduce fracture risk`、`works better than rock calcium`
- 不要碰：`AlgaeCal®`、`same as AlgaeCal`、exact protected source wording
- 多矿物、耐受性、吸收率等卖点都需要供应链或临床支持

## 7. Residual Risks & Required Review

- supplier spec / COA / trace mineral profile
- brand and trademark clearance
- exact source wording legal review
- final Amazon and label compliance review
