# Prompt Library

Use one of these starter prompts and adapt it to the actual case.

## Standard Safe-Rebuild

- `Use supplement-safe-rebuild to turn this upstream risk report into a Chinese safe-rebuild brief with 3 candidate directions.`
- `Read this ingredient-risk dossier and preserve the functional goal while avoiding branded ingredients, protected source stories, and risky claims.`
- `Read this Fatty15-style upstream dossier and preserve broad cellular wellness goals without reusing FA15(TM), essential-fatty-acid certainty, or longevity superiority language.`

## Bone Health Use Cases

- `Based on this AlgaeCal-style upstream report, keep the plant/algae calcium category cue where possible, but avoid branded source names and bone-density-growth claims.`
- `Give me 3 rebuild directions that keep the supplement facts logic close to the competitor's selling points without copying their branded implementation.`

## Chinese Starters

- `请基于上游风险报告做低风险功能重建，输出中文固定结构，并给 3 条方案。`
- `请保留功能目标和允许保留的品类特征，但主动避开 branded ingredient、exclusive story 和高风险 claim。`
- `请把可保留概念、必须替换项、必须避开项和待法律复核项分开写清楚。`
- `请基于 Fatty15 的上游报告，保留 broad cellular wellness 目标，但不要碰 FA15(TM)、fatty15、essential fatty acid 和 3X vs fish oil 叙事。`
