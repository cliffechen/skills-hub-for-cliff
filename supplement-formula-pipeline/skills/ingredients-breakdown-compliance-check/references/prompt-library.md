# Prompt Library

Use one of these starter prompts and then adapt it to the actual case.

## ASIN-Led

- `Use ingredients-breakdown-compliance-check to analyze ASIN B09ZQ74495 and output a Chinese fixed-structure ingredient-risk dossier.`
- `Use ingredients-breakdown-compliance-check to analyze ASIN B0DNBL1D2J and separate generic C15:0 chemistry from branded FA15(TM) and fatty15 story layers.`
- `Break this Amazon supplement into ingredient layers, classify each layer, and score TM / patent / compliance / marketplace risk.`

## Brand-Led

- `Analyze the brand AlgaeCal and separate generic nutrient logic from protected branded implementation.`
- `Review this supplement brand and tell me which ingredients or source stories look branded, proprietary, or unclear.`

## Ingredient-Line-Led

- `Split this line before judging risk: Calcium (as AlgaeCal(R) Mesophyllum superpositum) 750 mg.`
- `Tell me which parts of this ingredient line are generic, which are branded, and which require legal review.`

## Chinese Starters

- `请用 ingredients-breakdown-compliance-check 分析这个 ASIN，并输出中文固定结构报告。`
- `请先拆分嵌套成分行，再判断 trademark / patent / compliance / marketplace 风险。`
- `请把竞品里可保留的概念和应避开的品牌/专有层分开写清楚。`
- `请分析 B0DNBL1D2J，把 C15:0 成分层、FA15(TM) 品牌层、essential fatty acid/longgevity claim 层分开写。`
